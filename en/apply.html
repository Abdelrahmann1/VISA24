Get Invitation to Russia

Russian visa is a document which gives a foreign citizen a permission to cross the Russian borders with the purpose of transit or staying in Russia. In order to obtain a visa you need a pack of documents to submit to the Russian diplomatic authority of your country. Set of documents differs depending on a visa type. Visa is being pasted to a foreign citizen’s passport..

Apply For Russian Visa

Russian Visa Information

Most foreign citizens need a Russian Visa to travel to the Russian Federation. A Russian Visa is issued by the Embassy or Consulate of the Russian Federation in the applicant's country of residence.

The main document required for obtaining a Russian Visa is a Visa Invitation to Russia (also known as Visa Support). A Tourist Visa Invitation consists of two parts:

  • ✔️ Tourist Voucher
  • ✔️ Confirmation

The invitation contains our company name, address, and a Reference Number assigned by the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of Russia.

Steps to Obtain a Russian Visa:
  1. Fill in our online form to obtain a Visa Invitation to Russia.
  2. Complete the online visa application form on the official website of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of Russia and enter your visa invitation and personal details.
Need More Information? Visit the official webpage of the Russian Embassy in your country for complete visa requirements.

Who needs a visa?

Only citizens of those countries which don’t have a non-visa regime with Russian Federation need a visa.In order to enter the Russian Federation territory in the 2020, citizens of most countries need a visa. EU, North American and Chinese citizens are those who need a visa to Russia. There is a visa-free regime between Russia and some Near East, Asian and South American countries. They don’t need a visa to visit our country.

Types of Russian visas

There are different types of Russian visas which differ on purposes. The main detail for you is the length of stay on the Russian territory. Another important criterion is a number of entries and exits from Russian territory during a visa validation period. Visas can be for a single* or multiple entries. If you want to travel frequently between countries, then multiple entry visa is more convenient option. If you plan a single visit, it will be cheaper and simpler to issue a single entry visa.
