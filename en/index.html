    Dear guest,

    Welcome to Russia! To ensure a comfortable and safe stay, please review this guide. It contains important information about the rules of staying in the Russian Federation.

    1. Visa Validity
    - Your visa is issued for a specific period as indicated in the visa stamp. - Overstaying beyond the permitted period is not allowed and may result in administrative or criminal penalties. - If you need to extend your stay, contact the local office of the *Main Directorate for Migration Affairs (GUVM) of the Ministry of Internal Affairs of Russia* in advance. 2. Migration Registration
    - You must register within 7 business days of arrival in Russia. - If staying at a hotel or hostel, the administration will handle the registration. - If staying in private accommodation (e.g., with friends or relatives), the host must submit a notification to *GUVM MVD*. - Registration is mandatory, even for short stays. - If you change your place of stay for more than 7 business days, you must re-register at the new address. 3. Medical Insurance
    - You must have valid medical insurance for your entire stay in Russia. - Insurance covers emergency medical assistance in case of illness or accident. - It is recommended to arrange insurance before traveling or check with your tour operator. - **Without insurance, you will be responsible for all medical expenses.** 4. Prohibited Actions
    During your stay in Russia, it is *prohibited* to: - Overstay beyond the period specified in your visa. - Engage in employment without proper authorization (if you have a tourist visa). - Visit restricted areas (e.g., border zones, military facilities) without special permission. - Violate public order (e.g., hooliganism, drinking alcohol in public, traffic violations). - Give your passport or migration card to third parties. - Ignore requests from law enforcement officers (always remain calm and polite when asked to show documents). 5. Important Documents
    Always carry the following: - Your passport with a valid visa. - Migration card (issued upon entry to Russia). - Registration document (proof of migration registration). 6. Emergency Contacts
    - Police: **102** - Ambulance: **103** - Fire Department: **101** 7. Useful Tips
    - Keep copies of all important documents (passport, visa, registration) in electronic form. - Check entry and stay requirements before your trip. - If you have any questions or issues, contact your tour operator or *GUVM MVD Russia*. 8. Penalties for Violations
    - Violating stay regulations in Russia may result in *administrative penalties* (fines, deportation). - Serious violations (e.g., overstaying by more than 30 days) may lead to *criminal liability*.
