TERMS AND DEFINITIONS

Visa – permission issued (issued – in the part concerning the unified electronic visa) by an authorized state body to enter the Russian Federation and stay in the Russian Federation or transit through the territory of the Russian Federation on the basis of a valid document certifying the identity of a foreign citizen or stateless person and recognized by the Russian Federation as such. Depending on the purpose of entry of a foreign citizen or stateless person into the Russian Federation and the purpose of their stay in the Russian Federation, the foreign citizen or stateless person is issued a visa, which may be a diplomatic, service, ordinary, transit visa, temporary resident visa, or a unified electronic visa is issued in the form of an electronic document. The Regulation on establishing the visa form, the procedure and conditions for its registration and issuance, extension of its validity, restoration in case of loss, as well as the procedure for visa cancellation, was approved by the Decree of the Government of the Russian Federation of 09.06.2003 No. 335. The Regulation establishes the visa form issued to a foreign citizen, the procedure and conditions for its registration and issuance, extension of its validity, restoration in case of loss, as well as cancellation and extension to all categories of visas. If an international treaty of the Russian Federation establishes rules other than those provided for in this Regulation, the rules of the international treaty shall apply. The purpose of the foreign citizen's trip is indicated in the visa in accordance with the list of travel purposes used when registering and issuing visas to foreign citizens. The data specified in the visa, as well as information about the registration, issuance, extension of its validity, restoration or cancellation of the visa are visa information. A single-entry visa gives a foreign citizen the right to enter the Russian Federation once, a double-entry visa - the right to enter the Russian Federation twice, and a multiple-entry visa - the right to enter the Russian Federation multiple times (more than 2 times). The total period of stay of a foreign citizen in the Russian Federation on a multiple-entry visa is established when the visa is issued by the authorized state body, but not more than 90 days during each period of 180 days, except for cases provided for by this Regulation. An ordinary tourist visa is issued to a foreign citizen entering the Russian Federation as a tourist. An ordinary tourist visa can be single-entry or double-entry for up to 3 months, or multiple-entry for up to 6 months. An ordinary tourist group visa for a period of up to 1 month is issued to foreign citizens entering the Russian Federation as tourists as part of an organized tourist group (at least 5 people, but not more than 50). The following are mandatory conditions for issuing such a visa: all members of the group must travel in one vehicle, all members of the group must arrive at the border crossing point when entering and leaving the Russian Federation at the same time, stay at any point and travel within the territory of the Russian Federation only as part of a group. An ordinary tourist group visa can be single-entry or double-entry. An ordinary tourist group visa is issued by diplomatic missions and consular offices of the Russian Federation for organized entry into the Russian Federation of foreign tourists in exceptional cases due to difficulties in obtaining individual visas, and only with the permission of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the Russian Federation. An ordinary tourist visa is issued on the basis of confirmation of the acceptance of a foreign tourist by an organization, details of which are contained in the unified federal register of tour operators, issued in accordance with the procedure set out in the appendix to the Regulation, or on the basis of confirmation of a hotel reservation or other accommodation facility, details of which are contained in the unified list of classified hotels, ski slopes, beaches (hereinafter referred to as a hotel), issued in accordance with the procedure set out in the appendix to the Regulation. Visa support is a set of services for obtaining a visa (visas) and invitations to obtain a visa (visas) to the Russian Federation for foreign citizens. Embassy is a visa department of the Consulate of a foreign state or another division of the embassy of a foreign state authorized to consider requests for visas.
